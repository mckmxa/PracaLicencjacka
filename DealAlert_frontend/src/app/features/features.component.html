DealAlert's features

Your own wishlists

Accessible through navigation panel at the top of every page, your list of currently desired items

Price History Charts

Price history charts are another major part of dealalert. Graphs of the price variations over time are available through wishlist by clicking appropriate icon. Price and availability data is updated from Amazon constantly to ensure it is as accurate as possible, but should always be checked on Amazon before making a purchase.

 Price Drop Alerts

The primary feature of our site is the sending of email alerts when prices change. While in wishlist page, the user simply sets a price threshold at which alerts are generated, and we email you when that condition is met.

 Simple product search

Search is accessed via the box at the top of browse page.

 Safe registration

If you do choose to sign up or receive email alerts, we will not sell or distribute your information to anyone without your consent.
